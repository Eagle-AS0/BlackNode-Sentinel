# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, professional, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

BlackNode Sentinel is an open and inclusive project.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Give constructive feedback
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on collaboration

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments
- Personal attacks
- Spamming or trolling

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove comments, commits, or contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.
